My Manifesto

I'm running to make it easier for every student to find and do what they love, at Bath and beyond.

I've spent two years inside the Activities Office and know what works, what doesn't, and what students actually need. These are the commitments I'm making if you vote for me.click to edit

Student Groups

  • Develop storage hubs from under-used campus spaces
  • Improve facilities and capacity in Scala Hall
  • Better access to kitchen and communal eating spaces #LetEmCook
  • Clearer guidance for starting new groups and simplified admin
  • Provisional city venue bookings for major annual events like Balls
  • More drop-in sessions and clearer event-planning support #EventsWithEmily
  • Launch a Collaboration Calendar so committees can share ideas and find groups for joint activities
  • Stronger links between student groups and sports clubs through Bath Active
  • Promote funding and sponsorship to reduce financial barriers
  • Expand student leader rewards including campus discounts

Volunteering and Fundraising

  • Centralised SU fundraising calendar to support large events
  • Micro-volunteering and one-off opportunities so students can contribute flexibly

Sustainability

  • Dedicated funding for sustainable events, small grants to embed sustainability across activities
  • More affordable and plant-based food on campus (the Market needs to up its sandwich game)
  • Costume and props catalogue, Ball outfits and Halloween accessories with a small deposit to keep items circulating
  • Costume and show feature for dance and performing arts groups to cut fast fashion

Employability

  • Support Bath Insights and advocate for more structured on-campus paid roles
  • Tailored careers support for international students with the International and PG Officer

Wellbeing and Community

  • Year-round Housemate Finder platform including features for placement and study-abroad students
  • Reduce module assessment clustering by flagging overlapping deadlines in inter-disciplinary degrees

Hopes and Dreams

  • More microwaves and kettles on campus
  • Come find me on the Parade, free goodies guaranteed
