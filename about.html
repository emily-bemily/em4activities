About me
Hi! I'm Emily, Chair of the Activities Exec Committee, SU Activities Administrator of 2 years, and final year Social Policy student (which is a pretty relevant degree for an SU Officer role...). Through my work in the Activities Office, I've supported over 150 groups, helping committees run events, upload tickets, and survive the constant stream of questions that comes with being a student. I'm also a Lead Peer Mentor and SUmmit member. During my time at Bath, I've been involved in Dodgeball, Crochet and Knit, VegSoc, and salsa, which means I can throw things, make things, eat things, and dance (badly). Perfect skills for an Activities Officer. I'm a twin, a vegetarian, can crack an egg with one hand, and am currently learning to play the harmonica, because I believe in transferrable skills. I love sea swimming, meeting new people, and sleep, although realistically I manage about one of those during term-time.

Why I'm running
I've spent two years working in the SU Activities office learning the ins and outs of student life at Bath. It's my final year of university, and before I'm yeeted into the void post-graduation, I'd love to use what I've learned to give something back to the student communities that have shaped my life at Bath. And honestly, this is the one and only time where it is socially acceptable to go rogue on the Parade with my campaign team. So yes, I'm running. Come find me on campus for a yap and free goodies.
